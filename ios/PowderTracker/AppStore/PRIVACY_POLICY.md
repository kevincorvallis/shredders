# Privacy Policy for PowderTracker

**Last Updated: January 30, 2026**

## Introduction

PowderTracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address (when using email sign-up)
- Display name (optional)
- Profile preferences (favorite mountains, pass type)

**User Content**
- Events you create
- Comments and discussions
- Photos you upload
- RSVP responses

### Information Collected Automatically

**Location Data**
- We request location permission to show nearby ski resorts
- Location is used in real-time and is NOT stored on our servers
- The app works fully without location permission

**Usage Data**
- App interactions (screens viewed, features used)
- Crash reports and diagnostics
- Device type and iOS version

### Information from Third Parties

**Apple Sign In**
- If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID email (or relay email if you choose to hide it)
- We do not receive your Apple password

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide ski conditions and weather data
- Enable trip planning and social features
- Send powder alerts (if enabled)
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Respond to support requests

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT sell your personal information.**

We share data only with:
- **Supabase** - Our database and authentication provider
- **TelemetryDeck** - Privacy-focused analytics (no personal data shared)
- **Weather APIs** - We send location coordinates to weather services to retrieve forecasts (no personal identifiers)

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL with encryption)
- Authentication tokens are stored in iOS Keychain
- All network requests use HTTPS encryption
- We retain your data while your account is active

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Access** your data through the app's Profile section
- **Update** your profile information at any time
- **Delete** your account by contacting support@shredders.app
- **Opt out** of analytics in app settings

## Children's Privacy

PowderTracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:
- Email: support@shredders.app
- Website: https://shredders-bay.vercel.app/support
